Revamped the Site!

Oct 15, 2016 • Abdul-Jabbar Bozdar • This article will take 1 minute to read.

Tweet This Blog!

After two weeks, trying with different frameworks, I prefered to revamp my website using Jekyll.

I also tried to customise HTML/CSS and JavaScript to publish my blog oriented website. But, it was quite time consuming. It got almost impossible to automate certain blogging elments, like tagging and pagination. To save my time so, I could focus on my real work, Jekyll was the only best choice to implement my new website. At least, it is better, faster and simple than wordpress.

Keep visiting my Jekyll website!
